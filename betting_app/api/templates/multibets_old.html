About Multi-Bets

Multi-bets (parlays) combine multiple selections into one bet. All selections must win for the bet to pay out.

These suggestions include 2-leg to 6-leg combinations from the best value NFL and NBA picks based on historical data, sentiment analysis, and odds from multiple bookmakers.

Combined Value = (Combined Probability Ã— Combined Odds) - 1. Higher odds mean higher potential returns but lower probability.

Note: Each multi-bet only includes one selection per game to avoid correlation. Maximum odds limited to 1:100 for realistic opportunities.
